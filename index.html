Power of Change

End Injustice Now

We are in a critical moment. Institutions known of taking advantage of the BIPOC community are now being held responsible. The solidarity we're witnessing is a relentless group fighting for one thing, change. Changes that creates a more socioeconomic diversity and prohibit unfavourable treatment on the basis of ethnicity, nationality and race.

Silence Is Not An Option

We are witnessing a progressive movement toward change. Corporations, professional sports, state statues and academic institution are in the midst of reforming their images. This movement is speaking for the voiceless who have been muffled from this very issue for a very long time. We must acknowledge how far we've come but also how far we have to go as a society.
